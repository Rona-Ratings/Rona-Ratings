Purpose, Audience & goal

purpose: The purpose of this project will be a website that users can easily find restaurants around the Albuquerque area and see covid safety and ratings.

Audience:This site will be designed with an audience between the ages of 18-70 both male and female. Those who have underlying health issues that may be at risk, and also those who live with the elderly.

Goal: The primary goal for this site is to educate, connect, and provide covid statistics and safety for restaurants in the abq area

Persona

Name: Stephanie Garcia Age:28

Profession: Stephanie is a registered nurse, has no children but 2 dogs and lives alone. She is dedicated to her job and feels a sense of pride working in her field during the pandemic.

Technology: Windows 10 user, Dell XPS 15" laptop, 8gb ram, 9th gen Core i& processor. She uses an LG V40 from 2018 with a 32gb external memory card for pictures and music.

Attitudes & behaviors:Stephanie works long hours at the hospital and is adament about ensuring enough time for exercise. Being a nurse, she is naturally more cautious about Covid safety and keeps track of new and updates regarding it. a healthy diet and hardly ever gets sun light.

Frustrations and needs: Stephanie is frustrated when she sees people wearing masks incorrectly or not at all. Because of he work schedule and exercise routine she has little time to cook and needs to order out regularly.

Goals: Stephanie's goals are to quickly make judgments easilly on take out food and feel assured that it is handled appropriately. Her secondary goals are being informed about covid and are served by visualising the per/zip-code Covid numbers.

User Story

as a user, I need quick and easy information on local restaurants as well as casual Covid updates.

Use Case Interaction Flow

  • Stephanie access the website
  • Stephanie selects a food type from the dropdown and clicks a zipcode on the map
  • website returns a list of tiles, with images and information that is displayed on hover
  • Stephanie selects a tile
  • website returns a single listing page for the selected restaurant.
  • Stephanie browses information and decides to make a phone call to the restaurant.
