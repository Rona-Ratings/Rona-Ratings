Purpose, Audience, & Goal

Purpose: The purpose of this project will be a website that suers can easily find restaurants around the albuquerque area and see covid safety and ratings.

Audience: This site will be designed with an audience between the ages of 18-70 both male and female.Those who have underlying health issues that may be at risk, and also those who live with the elderly.

Goal : The primary goal for this site is to educate, connect, and provide covid statistics and safety for restaurants in the abq area
